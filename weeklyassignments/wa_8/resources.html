About CATSA

The Chinese and Taiwanese Student Association connects students interested in Chinese and Taiwanese culture. We provide resources, host events, and create a supportive environment for all students.

Our team collaborates with other campus organizations to offer language exchange, cultural workshops, and professional development opportunities.
